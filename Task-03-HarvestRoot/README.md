#Scenario
A premium organic food delivery service needed a modern, fresh visual identity that communicates trust, health, and speed through colors and typography.

#What I Did
- Created a Style Tile with a complete color palette and typography system
- Designed 3 key screens: Splash Screen, Product List, and Product Details
- Applied 8-point grid system for pixel-perfect spacing across all screens
- Used authentic "North Window Light" imagery direction natural, raw, not oversaturated

#Design System
| Element       |Choice           | Reason 
| Primary Color | Leaf Green      | Vitality & health 
| Secondary     | Earthy Brown    | Soil-to-table trust 
| Accent        | Harvest Gold    | Delight moments 
| Headline Font | Playfair Display| Editorial authority 
| Body Font     | Montserrat      | Clean readability 

#Key Insight
Avoided urgent reds entirely. Premium feel comes from restraint — using Harvest Gold sparingly for micro-interactions like the loading state and success confirmation.
